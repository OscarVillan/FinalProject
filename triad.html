Triad Park
The Patriot at Triad Park has a full set of 18 holes and features vast fields which are mowed weekly and large hills. The wooded areas are also kept in good shape and the whole design keeps you from being lost. There are also severl gravel paths that lead back to the main road. Racks can be found at every hole and there area few benches aswell. The Patriot features two difficulties of red and blue, red being the easier of the two with a lower par of 56. The blue tees offer a more difficult course and has a par of 59.
The Patriot was founded in 2015 and was designed by Russell Schwarz. It has expansive parking and there are park restrooms and waterfountains.
