Johnson St
The Johnson Street disc golf course has a full set of 18 holes and has a large parking lot able to fit at least 20 cars. This course always has players and is kept in very good shape, the fairways are always mowed and improvments are constantly being added. This course also features 3 difficulties ranging from white, blue and then gold. White being for beginners and blue for intermediates, gold tees are for experts and are significantly more challenging than blue. The course features open fairways and wooded areas along with a creek.
The course was founded in 1991 and designed by Chuck Rhoades and the TADGA (Triad Area Disc Golf Association). The par for the course is 54, 55, and 56 for white, blue and gold respectively. This course has the occasional tournements and sees regular use by local players.
